## Expressionist Style v3.0 — INTENTIONAL STROKES

### The breakthrough: fewer strokes, more meaning

Old approach: 966 dabs to fill the face → blurry mess
New approach: 38 intentional strokes → recognizable portrait

Each stroke has a PURPOSE:
- Face planes (light/shadow) → defines 3D form
- Eyes (iris, pupil, highlight, lid) → creates life
- Nose (bridge, sides) → creates depth
- Lips (upper, lower, line) → creates expression
- Hair (flowing directional) → creates mass
- Background (gestural) → creates context

### Value structure (the key to 3D form)

Instead of painting colors, paint VALUES:
- Shadow side: 0.75x brightness, pushed cool
- Light side: 1.15x brightness, pushed warm
- Highlights: 1.3x brightness
- Deep shadows: 0.4x brightness

This creates the LIGHT-SHADOW contrast that makes a flat image look 3D.

### Strokes per element

| Element | Strokes | Width | Purpose |
|---------|---------|-------|---------|
| Background | 8 | 80-200px | Gestural context |
| Face planes | 6 | 40-60px | 3D form |
| Nose | 3 | 6-8px | Depth |
| Eyes | 10 | 3-9px | Life |
| Lips | 3 | 2-12px | Expression |
| Hair | 8 | 15-18px | Mass |
| **Total** | **38** | | |

### What makes this REAL art vs previous attempts

1. **Selective emphasis** — face is detailed, background is abstract
2. **Value contrast** — shadows pushed dark, highlights pushed bright
3. **Color choices** — warm skin, cool background, red lips (not exact colors)
4. **Intentional marks** — every stroke has a role, none are filler
5. **Simplification** — 38 strokes say more than 10,000 dabs
