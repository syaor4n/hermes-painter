## Art Teachers — Constructive Evaluation System

### Why teachers > critics

Old critics (Monet, Rembrandt, Picasso, Van Gogh, O'Keeffe):
- Penalized for style preferences (Picasso: "too many strokes")
- Contradictory demands (minimalism vs expression)
- Average: 4.0/10

New teachers (Composition, Color, Form, Texture):
- Evaluate what's GOOD and give suggestions for improvement
- No contradictory demands
- Specific, actionable feedback
- Average: 6.7/10

### The 4 Teachers

| Teacher | Focus | What they see |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| Composition | Balance, focal, hierarchy | Focal contrast, position, visual hierarchy, depth |
| Color | Palette, harmony, mood | Palette size, value range, warmth, saturation |
| Form | Shapes, clarity, edges | Regional distinction, edge definition |
| Texture | Brushwork, surface | Brush texture %, surface variety, stroke direction |

### How to reach 8+/10

The main weakness is Composition (5.5 avg):
- Focal point needs more contrast/highlights
- Background should be simpler (fewer strokes)
- Visual hierarchy could be stronger

Fix: paint background with 50-80px strokes (very loose), focal with 10-15px strokes (tight detail).
